All-in-One: Customization & Security

AIP++ (Anti-Intruder Protection++) is a program designed to protect your device from physical intruders. It blocks access to applications that may pose a security threat.

Features

phonelink_lock
App Locker
close
Protects your apps from unauthorized access by showing the intruder a fake crash dialog.
  • When you try to launch an application protected by AIP++, the device displays an "error" message. In fact, there is no error, this is necessary to confuse the attacker.
  • To unlock the app, use the selected method. By default, this is a long press of the "About app" button.
  • In AIP++, you can configure different ways to unlock apps to ensure maximum security for your device.

phonelink_lock
Unlock Protection
close
Takes actions if someone fails to unlock your phone in 2 attempts.
  • If someone tries to unlock your device, but fails multiple times, the following actions can be taken:

    Alarm

    • The device will play an alarm sound (you can change it if you want) at 100% volume. Any attempts to change it will be prevented.

    Intruder photo

    • The device will take a photo from the front and back cameras.
    • You can configure the camera from which the photos will be taken.

    More actions will be added in the future.

  • Please note that this feature requires the device admin permission in order to work. This is needed to detect failed unlock attempts and take the required actions.
  • This app NEVER uses ANY permissions without your agreement, they are ONLY used for the listed purposes.

Tiles

Useful tiles for Quick Settings
Tiles are buttons in the Quick Settings of your device (the menu that you open by swiping down from the top of the screen).

Sleep

When disabled, disables sleep mode until enabled again.
warning Be careful, because if you forget to enable it, this can cause battery drain.
shortcut
Shortcuts
close
Useful shortcuts for hidden apps on your device
This feature will help you to quickly access almost any part of your device (such as hidden apps, system settings, or other apps).

Files

The built-in Files app is hidden on most devices. But if you want to access it, you can do that in this app! Just go to Shortcuts and press Add below the Files button. You can choose how to add the shortcut: as a shortcut, but with a watermark, or as a separate app, without the watermark.
